CREATING VALUE FROM WASTE

Corporate Social Responsibility: Creating Value from Waste

We are committed to being a 100% green company. This is achieved through continuous efforts to improve our operations and minimize our impact on the environment. Through our program, we recycle the debris and sawdust that would otherwise be thrown away. This program also creates jobs for the locals who collect the materials from other wood mills.
